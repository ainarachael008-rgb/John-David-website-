Fraud & Scam Prevention

John David Talent Advisory does not request cryptocurrency payments, gift cards, or unofficial financial transfers from candidates.

We do not guarantee employment in exchange for payment or request recruitment payments for interview access or stakeholder approval.

Optional paid professional services such as resume enhancement or career branding support are communicated transparently and are entirely separate from recruitment consideration.

Candidates are encouraged to independently verify recruitment communications and report suspicious activity impersonating our brand or representatives.

Privacy Policy

Candidate information submitted through this website is handled confidentially and used solely for recruitment, talent advisory, and professional communication purposes.

We do not sell candidate information. Information may only be shared with verified employers or hiring stakeholders when relevant to active recruitment opportunities.

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are implemented to help protect submitted information from unauthorized access or misuse.

Terms & Conditions

Submission of resumes or inquiries through this website does not guarantee interviews, employment, or placement opportunities.

All hiring decisions remain solely with the hiring organization or stakeholder.

Optional paid professional services, including resume enhancement and career branding support, are separate from recruitment consideration.
